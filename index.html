About Deborah Circle

We are a nonprofit dedicated to uplifting impoverished women in Ethiopia and providing mentorship to communities in the United States. Our mission includes education, faith-based support, and economic empowerment.

Mentorship Programs

Faith-based and community-driven mentorship initiatives available in multiple cities. Programs are structured like church gatherings, offering spiritual, emotional, and professional guidance.

Fundraising Campaigns

We actively raise funds to support women's education, healthcare, and business training in Ethiopia.

Our Podcast
