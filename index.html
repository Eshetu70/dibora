About Deborah Circle

We are a nonprofit dedicated to uplifting impoverished women in Ethiopia and providing mentorship to communities in the United States. Our mission includes education, faith-based support, and economic empowerment.

Our Vision

Providing microloans, training, and support to help women build sustainable futures.

Donate Now Join Us Learn More

Our Mission

To awaken and empower women to live for their God-given purpose by using their gifts and talents through mentorship.

To equip low-income women in Ethiopia with the tools, training, and financial support they need to start businesses, gain independence, and uplift their communities.

Mentorship for Women

We believe every woman has unique gifts and talents. Our program offers holistic mentorship—supporting women in all areas of life so they can discover their purpose, grow with confidence, and reach their full potential.

📱 Join Our Viber Community! Stay connected and inspired through our monthly women's mentorship meetings, special classes, and book club discussions.

In Partnership With Enat Bank & Enkuwa Tabra

  • 💰 Small, Low-Interest Loans to launch or grow women-led businesses
  • 🧭 Mentorship for business guidance and confidence
  • 📚 Training in Financial Literacy & Personal Development

Through our program women gain the tools to achieve financial independence, support their families, and become agents of change in their communities.
