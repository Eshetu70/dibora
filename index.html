About Deborah Circle

We are a nonprofit dedicated to uplifting impoverished women in Ethiopia and providing mentorship to communities in the United States. Our mission includes education, faith-based support, and economic empowerment.

Our Core Pillars

  • Women's Empowerment: We encourage women by fostering confidence, leadership skills, and self-sufficiency. Through workshops and support groups, we create safe spaces where women can build their voice and shape their future.
  • Education & Literacy: We provide educational resources, scholarships, and literacy programs to young girls and women in rural Ethiopia, ensuring access to quality education regardless of economic status.
  • Faith-Based Mentorship: Our U.S.-based mentorship programs offer spiritual guidance, life coaching, and community support rooted in Christian values, helping individuals grow in character and purpose.
  • Economic Empowerment: We support women in developing vocational skills, small business training, and access to microloans or startup resources — enabling them to generate income and break the cycle of poverty.
  • Community Development: By partnering with local churches, schools, and community leaders, we ensure a sustainable impact through collaborative initiatives that promote long-term growth and transformation.

Together, we are planting seeds of hope, opportunity, and change — one woman, one family, and one community at a time.

Who We Are

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to uplifting impoverished women in Ethiopia and mentoring underserved communities in the United States. Our mission is grounded in compassion, education, spiritual growth, and economic empowerment.

Our Mission

To inspire transformation by providing access to education, faith-based mentorship, and economic opportunities for women and families in need—both locally and internationally.

Our Vision

A world where every woman has the resources, support, and confidence to overcome poverty and lead with purpose.

Where We Serve

In Ethiopia, we focus on rural women—supporting education, literacy, and entrepreneurship. In the United States, we support immigrant and underserved communities with mentorship, leadership programs, and life skills.

What Makes Us Different

  • Faith-led programs that build both personal and spiritual growth
  • Education-focused initiatives tailored for long-term impact
  • Community partnerships that create lasting change
  • Supportive mentoring relationships across generations

Together, we are building a legacy of strength, dignity, and opportunity for women and families around the world.

Get Involved

How to get involved – mobile version.

Join us in our mission to uplift women, empower communities, and foster sustainable change in Ethiopia and the United States. Your involvement can make a real difference. Here are several ways you can participate:

1. Make a Donation

Your financial contribution helps fund scholarships, mentorship programs, vocational training, and faith-based outreach. Every dollar supports our mission of empowerment and education.

2. Volunteer Your Time

Whether it’s community outreach, virtual mentoring, or event support, we welcome volunteers of all backgrounds. Even a few hours a month can have a lasting impact.

3. Become a Mentor

Share your experiences and guide others through our structured mentorship program. Help young women and families grow in confidence, career skills, and faith.

4. Partner With Us

We welcome collaborations with churches, nonprofits, and businesses. Partner with us on events, sponsorships, or program development to expand our shared impact.

5. Spread the Word

Follow us on social media, share our mission with your network, and help us reach more women and communities in need.
