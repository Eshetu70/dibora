About Deborah Circle Ministry

Deborah‚Äôs Circle Ministry (DCM) is a faith-based nonprofit organization headquartered in Kansas, USA. It began as a mentorship initiative focused on helping women maximize their potential, live with purpose, and become agents of positive influence in their communities. Today, the ministry has expanded its reach to support low-income women in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia through targeted economic empowerment programs.

Our Core Pillars

Mentorship

We equip women to discover, develop, and live out their God-given potential through personal growth, spiritual enrichment, leadership development, and practical life strategies. Our programs foster holistic growth in both character and purpose.

Economic Empowerment

We empower low-income women in Ethiopia through financial literacy, personal growth coaching, and entrepreneurial training. By offering startup resources, we help women build sustainable income, achieve financial independence, and break the cycle of poverty.

Maternal Support

We support public hospitals' maternity wards in Addis Ababa by providing essential supplies to new mothers and their babies, helping ensure a healthy start to life.

  • Women's Empowerment: Building confidence, leadership, and self-sufficiency through workshops, training, and safe community spaces.
  • Education & Literacy: Offering scholarships and educational programs to girls and women in rural Ethiopia to promote equitable access to quality learning.
  • Faith-Based Mentorship: Delivering spiritual guidance and life coaching rooted in Christian values to help women grow in character and purpose.
  • Economic Empowerment: Supporting vocational skills development, microbusiness training, and access to startup funding.
  • Community Development: Partnering with local churches, schools, and leaders to promote long-term, sustainable impact through collaboration.
